## Get Help

### Email Support

For any questions, bug reports, or feature requests, reach out to us at [hello@lagomorph.ai](mailto:hello@lagomorph.ai). We're a small team and we read every email.

### Discord Community

Join our Discord server to chat with other Lagomorph users, share your creations, get tips, and connect directly with the dev team.

[Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/RsNwwy3WCc){: .btn .btn-primary }

---

## Bug Reports

Found a bug? Please report it via:

1. **Discord**: post in the #bug-reports channel (fastest response)
2. **Email**: send details to [hello@lagomorph.ai](mailto:hello@lagomorph.ai)

When reporting a bug, please include:

- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Your platform (Lagomorph Web / Unity version / Unreal Engine version)
- Screenshots or error messages if available
- Your browser (if using Lagomorph Web)

Have an idea for a feature? We want to hear it. Drop it in our Discord's #feature-requests channel or email us.
